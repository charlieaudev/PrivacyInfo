Privacy Policy — Bubble Popper Last updated: April 28, 2026 For terms of use for the App, see the App policy & terms (app-policy.html) if published alongside this file. This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Bubble Popper (the "App") handles information. By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App. Who this applies to The App is published on Apple’s App Store. The developer responsible for the App is identified on the App Store product page. For questions about this policy or the App, use the support or contact information shown on that page (or the email below, if provided). What the App stores on your device The App does not require you to create an account with the developer. Apart from optional Apple Game Center features described below, the App does not send your game data to developer-operated servers. The following may be stored only on your iPhone or iPad using standard on-device storage (for example, UserDefaults or similar APIs): • Settings — such as sound on or off, selected round length, and game speed. • High score records — for each finished game: your score, the round time limit for that game, and the date and time the game ended, so the App can show a local top-score list. We do not use this on-device data to identify you personally, and we do not receive a copy of it on our own systems through the App. What we do not collect through the App In the version covered by this policy, we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or precise location to play the App. We do not use the App to show third-party advertising. If that changes in a future version, this policy will be updated before or when those features go live. Apple and the App Store When you download, install, or update the App, Apple may process data (such as purchase and device-related information) under Apple’s Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/ — That processing is between you and Apple and is not described in detail here. Apple Game Center (optional) If you use Game Center (for example, to sign in, compare scores on leaderboards, or view friends’ entries), that activity is handled by Apple’s Game Center service, not by servers run by the developer. It is subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy (https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/) and applicable Game Center terms. You can play without Game Center; local high scores and settings work without it. • Sign-in — Game Center authentication is provided by Apple. The App does not receive your Apple ID password. • Leaderboards — when you are signed in, the App may submit your final round score to Apple for the leaderboard that matches the same round length and game speed you played, so it can appear on Game Center. • Friends — a friends-only view uses your Game Center friends as managed by Apple; the App does not build or host its own friend list. If you choose not to use Game Center, or sign out, those features are unavailable, but the rest of the App still works on your device. Children The App is a casual game. Parents and guardians who have questions are welcome to use the contact options on the App Store listing. Changes to this policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
